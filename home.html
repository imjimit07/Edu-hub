Welcome to Edu Hub!

What is Edu Hub?

Edu Hub is a non-profit online platform where students and teachers from any corner of the world can connect and where students can prepare for their exams

Ok! But what do I have to pay?

It's free! We believe that educationa should be feasable by one and all and thus we have waived off all charges for the users.

Oh wow, that's great but what will I, as a student, will get out of it?

If you are on Edu Hub, you will be able to avail the following services-

  • We know that different people have different way of learning and some do learn by listening and thus we have our text to speech service so that you can convert your book to an audio book. (For personal use only as we respect author's copyright policies.)
  • If you are the one who loves to learn by watching videos, go check out the our 'Edu Vlogs' section where you can get playlists of educational videos which are perfect for you.
  • Do you have a lot of doubts? No problem check our 'Have a doubt?' section . You can either search your doubt on our community platform to check if it is already answered or you can search it on internet. In case you still have doubts visit 'Edu community' section and post your question their which will be asnwered by a teacher or a student.

So, what are you waiting for? Sign up to our service now and do share your experience with us and your friends and family.
